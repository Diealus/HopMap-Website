1. Information We Collect 1/9

HopMap is designed to operate entirely on your device without collecting, storing, or transmitting any personal or user data to us or third parties. All processing‚Äîsuch as creating, editing, saving, and reviewing HopMap files (e.g., .HopMap or .json formats)‚Äîhappens locally in your device's memory or storage.
